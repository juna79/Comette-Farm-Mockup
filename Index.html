Our Story

Growing Good Food
Since 2008

Comette Farm began on a small plot in the Limuru highlands, where our founder Amara Wanjiku planted her first kitchen garden with seeds saved from her grandmother's farm. What started as a way to feed her family with honest, chemical-free produce quickly grew into something much larger.

Today we partner with over 40 small-scale farmers across the Central Highlands, Rift Valley and Coast regions of Kenya, bringing the finest seasonal produce, artisan dairy, handcrafted preserves and freshly cut herbs directly to homes across Nairobi. Every item is selected by hand, packed with care, and delivered at the peak of freshness.

We believe great food starts long before it reaches your plate — in the soil, in the relationship between farmer and land, in the seasons themselves. That philosophy drives everything we do.

🌱
Grown Sustainably
Every farm in our network uses regenerative practices — composting, crop rotation, water harvesting and zero synthetic pesticides. We farm for the next generation.
🤝
Fair to Farmers
We pay our farmers above-market prices because we know that fair partnerships produce better food. Over 85% of our farmers have been with us for five or more years.
🥬
Honestly Fresh
Our produce travels from farm to your door in under 24 hours. No cold storage, no additives, no compromises. Just honest, seasonal food at its absolute best.
